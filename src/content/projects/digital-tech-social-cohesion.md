# Utilizing Digital Technology for Social Cohesion, Positive Messaging and Peace by Boosting Collaboration, Exchange and Solidarity

**Funding Agency:** European Commission, EuropeAid

**Dates:** 2021-2025

**Note:** Bogazici University acted as a partner

This project aimed to utilize digital technology to promote social cohesion, positive messaging, and peace by fostering collaboration, exchange, and solidarity among diverse communities.
