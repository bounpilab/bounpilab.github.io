# Developing Multi-modal Neural Machine Translation Corpus and System for Low-Resource Languages

**Funding Agency:** Boğaziçi University Research Fund 20013

**Dates:** 2024

**Fund:** 360,000 TL

This project focused on developing a multi-modal neural machine translation corpus and system specifically designed for low-resource languages, combining text with other modalities to improve translation quality.
