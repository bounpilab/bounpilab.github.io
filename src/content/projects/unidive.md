# Universality, Diversity and Idiosyncrasy in Language Technology (UniDive)

**Funding Agency:** COST Action CA21167

**Dates:** 2022-2026

UniDive is a COST Action that brings together researchers working on language technology to address the challenges of universality, diversity, and idiosyncrasy in natural language processing across different languages and language families.
