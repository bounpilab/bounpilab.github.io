# Named Entity Recognition and Hashtag Segmentation in Social Media Text

**Funding Agency:** Bogazici University Research Fund, BAP

**Principal Investigator:** Arzucan Özgür

**Dates:** 2015-2017

This project developed methods for named entity recognition and hashtag segmentation specifically designed for the challenges of social media text, including informal language, abbreviations, and hashtags.
