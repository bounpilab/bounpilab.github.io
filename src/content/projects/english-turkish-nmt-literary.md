# Developing English-Turkish Neural Machine Translation Methods in the Literary Domain

**Funding Agency:** Boğaziçi University Research Fund 19986

**Dates:** 2024-2026

This project focuses on developing neural machine translation methods specifically tailored for literary texts in the English-Turkish language pair. Literary translation presents unique challenges compared to general domain translation, including preserving stylistic elements, cultural nuances, and authorial voice.
