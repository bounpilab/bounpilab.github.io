Welcome to PILAB

PILAB is a research group at Bogazici University focused on developing novel methods in computer vision, biometrics, and 3D visual understanding.

Computer Vision

We research visual recognition, image understanding, object detection, and scene analysis, with applications in autonomous systems and visual AI.

Biometrics

We develop methods for face recognition, person re-identification, and biometric authentication using deep learning techniques.

3D Computer Vision

We work on 3D reconstruction, depth estimation, and geometric understanding of visual data to bridge the gap between 2D imagery and 3D scenes.

Our research combines deep learning with geometric and perceptual principles to build robust, real-world visual intelligence systems.
