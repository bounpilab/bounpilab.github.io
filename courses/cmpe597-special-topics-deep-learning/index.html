Deep Learning

CMPE 597

Instructor: Assoc. Prof. İnci Baytaş

Course Description

An introduction to artificial neural networks, deep learning, fundamental deep architectures, and their applications.

Topics (official, summarized)

  • Multilayer perceptron
  • Training deep networks and optimization
  • Convolutional neural networks
  • Sequence modeling (RNNs, transformers)
  • Graph neural networks
  • Auto-encoders
  • Deep generative models (VAEs, GANs, diffusion models)
