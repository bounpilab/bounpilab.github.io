Language in the Human-Machine Era (LITHME)

Funding Agency: COST Action CA19102

Dates: 2020-2024

Role: Secondary Proposer

LITHME is a COST Action that explores how language is evolving and will continue to evolve in the era of increasing human-machine interaction, including the impact of AI, voice assistants, and machine translation on human communication.
